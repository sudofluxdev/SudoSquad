## Usage

1. **Engage**: @TheManager activates @TheArtist & @TheArchitect.
2. **Review**: Check Mockups vs Implementation.
3. **Audit**:

    - **Spacing Systems** (8px grid)
    - **Typography** (Scale, Weight)
    - **Animations** (Transitions, Feedback)
4. **Refine**: Implement Tailwind classes for pixel perfection.
